### F-Script 注入
事先将FScript.framework拷贝到/Library/Frameworks，然后使用gdb


```
attach xxx
p (char)[[NSBundle bundleWithPath:@"/Library/Frameworks/FScript.framework"] load]
p (void)[FScriptMenuItem insertInMainMenu]
continue
```
